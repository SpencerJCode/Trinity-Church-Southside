Our Elders
Our elders, from left to right: Spencer, Korey, and Hunter.
Spencer and his precious bride were married in 2017, and have three children. He is a full-stack developer at the Homeschool Legal Defense Association, and his bride is a homemaker, piano teacher, and dog-breeder. His spiritual influences include Greg Koukl, Ralph Erskine, John Bunyan, J.R.R. Tolkein, and John Piper.
Korey was wed to Crystal in 2009, and the Lord has blessed and entrusted them with a couple handfuls of kids. Together they sing and read and build things in the place they call Beulah Land.
"Now I saw in my dream, that, by this time, the pilgrims were got over the Enchanted Ground, and entering in the country of Beulah, whose air was very sweet and pleasant; the way lying directly through it, they solaced themselves there for a season" (John Bunyan, Pilgrim's Progress).
Hunter married Sara in 2011 and they currently have 7 children (6 sons, 1 daughter). They moved to Southern VA from TN in 2020, and he serves the community as a plumber by trade, while Sara beautifies their home. They enjoy singing and stories together while establishing their homestead.
